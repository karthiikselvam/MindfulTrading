Program Description:

Learning how to trade is not, and never will, be easy. Trading is a huge challenge for all of us, as it causes frustration and a feeling of helplessness. Trying to learn how to trade on your own is not for everyone.If you are looking for help in this process, stop learning multiple strategies, commit to learning a single one and become number one at it. If you are such a person, and want a guided process with private coaching that monitors your progress, this mentorship program is for you.

Learn how to make your own analysis and trading decisions by learning how to automatically correct yourself while following mechanical rules that will prevent you from making silly mistakes. You will see improvements in different areas of trading, such as understanding price actions, locating the turning points, as well as with your mindset and emotional control.Having a personal trading coach, that you trust, will allow you to really focus on your trading and learn a great deal about yourself, while someone else guides you on your trading journey.

Private coaching for 12 months includes:

1)12 months monitoring your progress and your understanding of the rules closely.

3)Learn Price action based supply and demand strategy that works.

4)Learn about entries, exits and risk control.

5)Top down analysis of live Stocks,Commodity and Forex scenarios, or any other market. The strategy works on all markets.

6)Unlimited personal support by email. Your analysis and doubts will be solved by email in just a few hours. If needed, replies will be recorded in video for a better understanding.

7)You get constant personal attention from Karthik Selvam, as well as one-on-one feedback on weekly basis as per your needs.
